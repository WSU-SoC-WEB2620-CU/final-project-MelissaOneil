Driving Directions

From Salt Lake City

Take the 24th St. exit off I-15 and turn right onto 24th Street. Continue over the bridge and turn right at Lincoln Ave, Then take your first right at the light onto 25th street, and a right again at Wall Ave. The venue is located on the right side of the street.

From North Ogden

Take the 21st St. exit off I-15 and turn left onto 21st Street. Continue for 2 1/2 miles, then turn right at Wall Ave. Continue on Wall Ave for 4 blocks, the venue is located on the left side of the street.

Parking

Parking is available at the venue, there is a lot behind the building and the entrance is on the South side of the building. Watch for "Party Parking" signs. There is also parking space cross the street in the Union Station Parking lot.
